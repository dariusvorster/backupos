# Health score

The health score is a 0–100 number (displayed as A–F) that summarises how healthy a backup setup is. It's computed per agent and per repository, refreshed after every run.

## Where the score appears

The score is shown alongside agents and repositories in the UI as a coloured grade badge. Use it as an at-a-glance signal: a sudden drop from A to C usually means several recent runs failed, or verification has started failing.

In V1, alerting on a health-score threshold is not yet a built-in alert trigger — the available triggers are growth spikes, cost thresholds, integrity errors, no-recent-backup, and approaching-limit. To act on health changes, watch the dashboard or rely on the underlying alerts (which fire on the symptoms — e.g. `no_recent_backup` — that drag the score down).
