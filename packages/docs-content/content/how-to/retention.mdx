# Tighten retention to save costs

Go to **Repositories → [repository] → Retention policy**. Configure the Restic forget policy:

<RetentionPolicy />

<Warning>Pruning actually reclaims storage — without prune, forget only removes the index entry. Enable **Run prune after forget** in the retention settings to ensure storage is freed.</Warning>

<Tip>Start conservative and tighten later. It's safer to keep more snapshots initially and reduce once you've confirmed restore works end-to-end.</Tip>
