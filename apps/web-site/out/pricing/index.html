Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate from self-hosted to cloud?
Yes. BackupOS Cloud uses the same Restic repository format. Export your repos, point them at your cloud workspace, and your snapshot history is intact.
What counts as "bundled storage"?
Bundled storage is the compressed, deduplicated data stored in your BackupOS-managed object bucket. You can also bring your own S3-compatible bucket at no extra cost.
Is the self-hosted version truly unlimited?
Yes — MIT license, no call-home, no feature flags. Repositories, users, and retention policies are all uncapped.
Can I cancel my cloud plan anytime?
Yes. Cancel with one click and export all your data within 30 days. No lock-in.
Do cloud plans include the DR restore wizards?
Yes. File, database, and full-host restore wizards are available on all cloud tiers.
How is repository password security handled on cloud?
Repository passwords are encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM before being stored. BackupOS Cloud never sees your plaintext keys.
